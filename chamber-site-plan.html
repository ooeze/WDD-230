Aba Chamber of Commerce

Fostering Growth, Community, and Collaboration

Site Name

Aba Chamber of Commerce was selected because it represents the city of Aba, an entreprenurial city and aligns with the purpose of fostering community and economic growth. The name is simple, professional, and recognizable.

Site Purpose

  • To serve as a hub for local businesses to connect and collaborate.
  • To provide information on events, resources, and economic development opportunities in Aba.

Scenarios

  1. What networking events are happening this month?
  2. How can I become a member of the chamber?
  3. Where can I find resources for starting a business in Aba

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #003366 (used for headers and navigation background).
  • Secondary Color: #FFCC00 (used for accents and buttons).

Typography

  • Heading Font: "Roboto", sans-serif (used for titles and headings).
  • Body Font: "Open Sans", sans-serif (used for body text).
